# CONTEXT: step8_internal_linking

## Workflow Position
This is **Stage 8** of the 12-stage SEODOMINATOR pipeline.
- **Input:** Article from Step 7, Existing published posts from DB
- **Output:** Article with internal links + list of DB updates for backward links.

## Goal
Bidirectional internal linking engine. Link this article to old posts, and update old posts to link to this one.

## Standard of Excellence
The single biggest lever for topical authority. Links must be highly contextual and natural. Anchor text must be varied and relevant.

## Agent Instructions
When working on this folder, ensure your code perfectly receives the input from the previous stage and outputs a cleanly typed dataclass/schema for the next stage. Do not bleed responsibilities across stages.
